What is Choons?

Choons is a revolutionary music platform that puts power back in the hands of real music lovers and artists. No more faceless algorithms deciding what's worth hearing. No more pay-to-play schemes that favor the already established.

With Choons, real people review and rate artists, tracks, and gigs to collectively decide what deserves attention. We're creating a community-driven music ecosystem where quality rises to the top naturally.

How it Works

🎵

Discover

Find new music across your favorite genres that has been highly rated by real music lovers, not pushed by algorithms or paid promotions.

⭐

Rate & Review

Share your thoughts on tracks and gigs. Your honest reviews help great artists get discovered and build their audience organically.

🎸

Connect

Follow your favorite artists, discover their upcoming gigs, and connect with other music lovers who share your taste.

📱

Stay Updated

Receive notifications when you get reviewed, when artists you follow announce gigs, or when your favorite genres feature new highly-rated tracks.

About the Creator

I'm an independent developer with a burning passion for music. After years of frustration with how major streaming platforms prioritize commercial success over musical quality, I decided to build something different.

Choons is completely bootstrapped—no venture capital, no outside investors demanding profits above all else. This means we're free to focus on what truly matters: creating an authentic platform that serves musicians and music lovers.

I built Choons because I believe music discovery should be driven by genuine human connection and appreciation, not by algorithms optimized for engagement metrics and ad revenue.

Choons will always be 100% free because music is for everyone.
